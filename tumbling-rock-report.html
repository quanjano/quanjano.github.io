tumbling rock cave in north alabama. saltpeter in the caves were used to make gunpowder in the civil war. about 2 or 3 miles into the cave is the king's shower, technically the tallest waterfall in alabama, at about 400ft. 5 miles in is a 200ft mountain of mud and stone called mount olympus. it is a slippery climb and very steep. at the top is the cave register, caked in mud, stowed in a pvc tube. my name is in there somewhere.
